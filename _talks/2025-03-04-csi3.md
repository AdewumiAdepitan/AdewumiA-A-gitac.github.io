I presented **Eco-driving** as part of *Session 5: Civil Infrastructure Systems* at the CSi3 Symposium (University of Miami).

**What I spoke about (brief):**
- Why eco-driving matters for **fuel efficiency, emissions reduction, and smoother traffic flow**
- Practical driver behavior factors (e.g., speed stability, harsh acceleration/braking)
- How eco-driving connects to **transportation sustainability and system performance**
